Building a Budget 101

Before you start doing a deep dive into the fascinating world of personal finance - everything from churning credit cards to trading options, the first step is to build a budget. At this stage, your budget doesn't have to be super complicated. But at a minimum, coming out of this exercise, you should be able to:

1. Identify how much you spend

2. Identify how much you make

3. Add up how much you have saved up

I've added a downloadable template that can help you calculate some of these numbers on your own. The instructions in the workbook should be pretty clear if you want to jump ahead, but see below if you want additional details on how to think through budgeting.
