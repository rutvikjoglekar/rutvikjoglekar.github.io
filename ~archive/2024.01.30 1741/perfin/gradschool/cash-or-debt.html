Saving cash or applying it to your debt

This is an MVP of a tool that can be used to calculate an estimate of the opportunity cost of holding cash in a savings account versus applying it to your debt. More detailed instructions to come soon, but a summary of the instructions are below.

Download the workbook here.

In the blue boxes, enter:

1. Extra cash you have available right now

2. Your state of residence

3. Your income

4. Tax filing status

5. Interest earned on savings (APY)

6. Principal balance on student loans

7. Interest paid on student loans (APR)

In the green boxes, enter the following optional fields for a more precise estimate:

1. Additional income

2. Term length of student loans

3. Pre-tax 401k contributions

4. Other, above-the-line deductions

5. Other, below-the-line deductions

6. Other state tax deductions
