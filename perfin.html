Personal finance

THIS PAGE IS A WORK IN PROGRESS.

Navigating personal finance is really, really hard! No one teaches us about this stuff as we grow up.

So I built this page out with a lot of information that I've learned since I graduated college, and these are the exact things I would do if I were to step back into the shoes of 22-year-old me. For now, it'll be mostly geared toward recent graduates starting out their careers.

A lot of this can seem convoluted and confusing on first glance, but I promise it isn't!
